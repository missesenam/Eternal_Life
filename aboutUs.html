Eternal Life Leadership Training Institute (ELLTI) is an Independent, not-for-profit Christian Leadership Training Institute founded by Apostle Thomas Appiah-Kusi in 2021. The Institute is located at Agbogba in the Greater Accra Region of Ghana.

Vision Statement

Our vision is “To be recognized as a global leader in cultivating exceptional leadership talent and fostering positive societal change through transformative training and development programs."

Mission Statement

Our mission is to empower individuals with the knowledge, skills, and mindset necessary to become visionary leaders who drive innovation, inspire others, and create lasting positive impact in their organizations and communities."
