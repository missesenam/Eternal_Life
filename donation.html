Support ELLTI’s Mission

At Eternal Life Leadership Training Institute (ELLTI), we believe that every individual has the potential to become a leader and bring transformative change through the teachings of Jesus Christ. Your support empowers us to offer high-quality, accessible Christian leadership training to students from all backgrounds. By investing in their education, you are helping to shape the future of Christianity and ignite a revival of faith.

Through free ministry training at ELLTI, students gain the knowledge and skills necessary to become impactful ministers, pastors, evangelists, and church leaders. Equipped with this training, they go on to serve their communities, spreading God’s Word, offering spiritual guidance, and bringing hope to those in need. Together, we are building a stronger foundation of faith and leadership for the Church and the world.
